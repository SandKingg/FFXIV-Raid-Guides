	each edge has two of one animal and three of another
echoed serenade - indicates an order for the animals to do line aoes
	dodge between the rows and columns as necessary

sunken treasure - summons a watery doughnut in each corner and two watery orbs either north/south or east/west
	all doughnuts and one orb will begin to crack, indicating that they will soon explode
hydrobullet - two random players get spread aoe markers above their heads
	they need to go into different doughnuts, next to the non-cracked orb
	the other two players can go to either
	finally the second orb will crack, so get away from that afterwards

hydrocannon - a line aoe tankbuster

aqua spear - creates aoes on five tiles, such that each row and column has one
sea shackles - tethers supports to dps that need to be kept a certain distance apart, indicated by the markers
tidal wave - a massive knockback from one edge of the arena, indicated by a big splash of water
	the knockback takes you exactly 3 tiles so position in such a way that you avoid the aoes and stay far apart
	then there's another knockback from a different, adjacent edge
	try to turn around without bringing the tethers too close
