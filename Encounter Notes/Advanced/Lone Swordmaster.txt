malefic quartering - gives everyone a compass debuff indicating which side they cannot take damage from
malefic portent - a tether that will give an extra 'bad side' to whoever takes it, indicated by the green arrows
shifting horizon - a north/south cleave followed by an east/west cleave or vice versa
unyielding will - directional slashes home in on players from the edge
	position yourself so that the slash hits you on a good side

near to/far from heaven - marks one player with a red marker and indicates either one or two swords around the boss
	either does a point-blank or a doughnut, based on the cast
	then the boss jumps to the marked player doing an enumeration based on the number of swords
	if the enumeration is wrong, players get thrown into the wall
	at the same time, the opposite aoe happens from where the boss started

echoing heat - three sets of large baited puddles underneath every player
	these leave behind puddles for a while so place them intelligently
wolf's crossing - indicates a plus aoe from where the boss was when she cast it
	this goes off with the second set of puddles
echoing eight - large baited puddles underneath every player
	these then do star aoes, so give yourself space to dodge

	these bad sides must be adjacent or you cannot complete the next mechanic
cardinal horizons - the same shifting horizon cleaves from earlier, but they spawn slashes at the edge
