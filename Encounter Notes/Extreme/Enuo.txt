meteorain - raidwide

naught grows - does a large arrow tether towards the void portal, doing either a big point-blank aoe or a big doughnut aoe from it, based on the marker
	also either one or both healers will be tethered to a moveable portal which does a line stack afterwards
	this is a wild charge so the tank/s should be closest to the portal
	make sure to split into light parties and give everyone enough space if there are two portals
	have a priority for which party goes which direction from the portal

naught wakes - moves the void portal to one of the three south markers

meltdown - baits an aoe under each player, as well as giving them a pyretic for 2 seconds, then does a spread aoe on every player
	bait mid, then wait for the debuff to wear off before going max melee to clock spots

airy/dense emptiness - either pair stacks on all supports/dps or light party stacks on both healers

naught grows - both the single/double portal and doughnut/point blank are opposite of the previous

gaze of the void - creates a helix of void orbs that shoot out cone aoes one by one
	the highest glowing one is where it starts, so keep rotating until you reach the first part that isn't hit twice
	each orb then creates another orb on the outside of the arena, with the first two being larger
	four of them have yellow timers and move quickly, the other four have purple timers and move slowly
	these orbs need to be soaked in pairs, yellow first, then purple, with tanks taking the big orbs
	assign a priority for who soaks which orbs

vacuum - creates eight small, curved line aoes from mid, some shorter and some longer
	void orbs fly to the ends of the aoes, before exploding
	stay out of the lines then quickly dodge the point-blank aoes from the orbs
dense/airy emptiness - opposite of the previous
	give yourself enough time to move into the correct spot after dodging vacuum

deep freeze - flare tankbusters on the top two in aggro, followed by a deep freeze debuff
	OT needs to be second in aggro here
	make sure everyone is moving when the flares go off until the debuff wears off
