piercing plunge - raidwide

familiar call - summons an array of animals on each edge
	there will be two pufferfish either north/south or east/west, with the other having two chocobos
	the rest will be either crabs or seahorses, such that each row or column has exactly one animal
echoed serenade - indicates the order in which each animal does their aoe
	crabs and seahorses do line aoes
	pufferfish do massive point-blank aoes
	chocobos do massive cone aoes
	there will be spread aoes on every player that go off with the 2nd and 4th aoes
	assign a corner for each player to dodge in, and stay close to the edge between the corner tile and an adjacent one for the point-blank and cone aoes

surging current - hits two diagonally opposite quadrants with aoes, followed by the other two quadrants

alluring order - a raidwide that gives everyone a forced march debuff, and two players a stack debuff
swimming in the air - flies around the arena, planting aoes everywhere except two corners
	the two stacks need to go to two different safe corners, so assign priorities and flex pairs

ceaseless current - creates exalines on two adjacent edges that propagate through the arena
	then every player gets a green marker that creates a plus aoe once the exalines reach the centre of the arena
		this means every tile will get hit once
surging current - this time the aoes hit north/south or east/west, followed by the opposite
	stay near your corner and give melees priority with staying close to the boss
	ranged stay all the way in the corner and dodge
	melees can actually stay in the middle of the arena, then quickly move to their tile for the plus aoes, then move back middle

raidwide

alluring order
sunken treasure - summons a watery doughnut in each corner and two watery orbs either north/south or east/west
	all doughnuts and one orb will begin to crack, indicating that they will soon explode
	use the forced marches with the same corner and flex pair priorities as before
	remember to dodge the second exploding orb afterwards
