piercing plunge - raidwide

familiar call - summons an array of animals on each edge
	there will be two pufferfish either north/south or east/west, with the other having two chocobos
	the rest will be either crabs or seahorses, such that each row or column has exactly one animal
echoed serenade - indicates the order in which each animal does their aoe, it will always be one of each
	the first two animals will be one of crab/seahorse and oen of chocobo/pufferfish in either order
	the last two animals will be the other two, in either order
	crabs and seahorses do line aoes
	pufferfish do massive point-blank aoes
	chocobos do massive cone aoes
	there will be spread aoes on every player that go off with the 2nd and 4th aoes
	assign a corner for each player to dodge in, and stay close to the edge between the corner tile and an adjacent one for the point-blank and cone aoes

surging current - hits two diagonally opposite quadrants with aoes, followed by the other two quadrants

alluring order - a raidwide that gives everyone a forced march debuff, and two players a stack debuff
swimming in the air - flies around the arena, planting aoes everywhere except two corners
	the two stacks need to go to two different safe corners, so assign priorities and flex pairs

ceaseless current - creates exalines on two adjacent edges that propagate through the arena
cross current - then every player gets a green marker that creates a plus aoe once the exalines reach the centre of the arena
	this means every tile will get hit once
surging current - this time the aoes hit north/south or east/west, followed by the opposite
	stay near your corner and give melees priority with staying close to the boss
	ranged stay all the way in the corner and dodge
	melees can actually stay in the middle of the arena, then quickly move to their tile for the plus aoes, then move back middle

raidwide

alluring order
sunken treasure - summons a watery doughnut in each corner and two watery orbs either north/south or east/west
	all doughnuts and one orb will begin to crack, indicating that they will soon explode
	use the forced marches with the same corner and flex pair priorities as before
surging current - another north/south or east/west cleave, stay on the correct side of the doughnut
	remember to dodge the second cleave and then the second exploding orb afterwards

aqua spear - creates an X pattern of tile aoes while giving everyone a tile spread marker that places an extra tile aoe
	assign cardinals for everyone to stay in, then place your aoe in the relative back left tile
	this makes a rough 'L' pattern
familiar call 2 - summons an array of crabs, seahorses and turtles
	one animal shoots through the middle, leaving the back side tile safe
	another animal shoots across the edges, leaving the front tile safe
	the final animal shoots in a # pattern, leaving the back centre tile safe
echoed serenade - this time the three animals are in a random order, but the fourth is always the same as the first
	dodge the aoes, but remember the order of the animals for later
aqua ball - you can place all three aoes in the same tile if you're clever
cross current - make sure to stand in the back side tile here

familiar call 3 - crabs, seahorses and turtles again but in a different pattern
echoed reprise - the animals shoot in the same order as before, but now their positions are different so watch out
surging current - shoots towards the quadrants this time

sea shackles - shackles players together in two random pairs with either a close or far tether
	also one player in each shackle pair will get a spread debuff
	preposition in such a way to make it easier to adjust for who goes where
sunken treasure 2 - this time creates doughnuts at each of the corners and orbs closer to the middle
	all doughnuts and two orbs will crack, meaning that the safe spots are the doughnuts near the two uncracked orbs
aqua ball - drop the three puddles and then move into the doughnut
	remember to dodge the second set of orbs afterwards!
