steelsbreath release - raidwide

near to/far from heaven - marks one player with a red marker, two players with 1 and 2 markers, and indicates either one or two swords around the boss
	the 1 marker either does a point-blank or a doughnut around them, respectively, based on the cast
	then the boss jumps to the marked player doing an enumeration based on the number of swords
	if the enumeration is wrong, players get thrown into the wall
	at the same time, the 2 player does the opposite aoe from before

malefic quartering - gives everyone a compass debuff indicating which sides they cannot take damage from
	either two players will have their north safe, and two their south, or the same but east/west
shifting horizon - either a north/south cleave or an east/west cleave that spawn slashes on the outside
	if the safe sides were north/south, then the cleave will be east/west and vice-versa
	position yourself on the same side as your safe spot
malefic portent - two tethers spawn that will give an extra 'bad side' to whoever takes them, indicated by the green arrows
	the tethers will always spawn one per pair of players with the same safe side, and the bad side it gives will always be the one safe side left
unyielding will - directional slashes home in on players from the edge
	position yourself so that the slash hits you on your good side
	meanwhile the tethers need to be taken by the players not targeted for unyielding will

echoing orbit/eight - spawns a big point-blank aoe underneath every player
	these then turn into either doughnut aoes or star aoes respectively, based on the cast
waiting wounds - three sets of three aoes that go off in the order they spawn
	move from the third set into the first once it goes off

near to/far from heaven

silent eight - gives every player a big spread aoe that spawns a star aoe after it goes off
	place these in the corners, then run back to mid while dodging the stars
maw of the wolf - turns towards a random player, then does a big half-room cleave in front of her

fangs of the underworld - a big multi-hit line stack

raidwide - this also creates four unsafe rectangles, making the aoe into a sort of shuriken shape

malefic quartering 2 - this time everyone gets two safe sides that are always opposite each other
	two players get north/south safe and two get east/west safe
	try to roughly position near one of the safe 'points' of the shuriken that matches one of your safe sides
steel horizon - either a north/south cleave followed by an east-west cleave, or vice versa
	these also spawn both big and small slashes on the sides of the arena
silent heat + unyielding will + will of the underworld - everyone gets a spread aoe, as well as a directional arrow from the small slashes
	you need to place your spread aoe in one of the 'points' of the shuriken while also making sure that you're not getting hit on a bad side by either the big slashes or the small slash tethered to you
	always go into a safe spot that matches one of your safe sides, to reduce the chance of collisions
	i.e. north/south safe sides will go north and south, and same for east/west
	if you get tethered to one of the small slashes that matches your safe side (e.g. a north/south player gets tethered to the north or south slash) then you MUST go into the safe spot directly opposite it
	otherwise, you can choose which spot to go to, but don't accidentally take away a safe spot from someone who would need to go there
