mousse mural - raidwide

single style (bombs) - draws three bombs at the indicated points on the arena, that explode into point-blank aoes

single style (winged bombs) - draws three winged bombs at the indicated points on the arena, that jump over to the other side before exploding

single style (arrows) - draws eight arrows at the indicated points on the edges, that do line aoes through the arena
	these will always be on adjacent edges so the aoes intersect, make sure to stand in a safe spot

single style (target) - draws a target in the middle of the arena, either facing north/south or east/west
single style (arrows) - draws twelve arrows on the edges facing the target, that do line aoes towards the target
	you can get melee uptime here if the boss doesn't move

pudding party - a multi-hit stack marker

color riot - aoe tankbusters on both tanks

sugarscape - transforms the arena into a desert

layer (sun) - paints the sun onto the landscape, giving everybody a small, permanent dot that lasts until the desert fades

layer (cacti) - paints cacti onto the landscape, creating expanding cacti on the arena that explode into large point-blank aoes

layer (quicksand) - paints quicksand onto the landscape, creating a large quicksand aoe that pulls anybody caught in it down to their deaths
	the quicksand pit only leaves the four corners safe, to the party should all go towards one of them
	the pit then moves to be centred at the midpoint of one of the edges, and more cacti spawn
	dodge the cacti while staying out of the quicksand
mousse touch-up - spread aoes on every player
	these go off after the cacti stop exploding

raidwide - returns the arena to normal
tankbusters

double style - paints two out of the four styles seen previously: bombs, winged bombs, arrows, or target
	the target will always be painted with arrows, but arrows can also be paitned with bombs or winged bombs

double style - a different pair from the previous

pudding party

sugarscape 2 - turns the arena into a riverscape, the river gives fire vuln down and lightning vuln up

taste of fire/thunder - paints either fire or thunder
	fire does stacks on both healers, make sure to stand in the river
	thunder does spread aoes on every player, don't stand in the river

taste of thunder/fire - opposite of the previous
raidwide

layer (rain) - paints a storm onto the landscape, making the river deadly and doing several sets of small point-blank aoes to dodge

layer (thundercloud) - paints a thundercloud onto the landscape, creating a tempest that moves around the arena
	highlightning x3 - a massive point-blank aoe from the tempest, stay away from it
		the tempest moves after each highlightning cast, and the point-blank aoes begin again

raidwide - returns the arena to normal
tankbusters

loops from double style until defeated
