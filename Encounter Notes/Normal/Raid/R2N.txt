	honey beeline - a line aoe through her
	tempting twist - a doughnut aoe centred on her

honeyed breeze - cone aoe tankbusters on both tanks

honey b. live - enters into a mini-phase, doing a raidwide and giving out heart stacks
	reaching three heart stacks stuns you and gives a vuln
	everyone starts out on zero hearts

love me tender - a spread aoe on each player that gives them a heart stack
	then spawns four towers that give a heart stack to anyone who stands inside them
	if not taken, they explode and give a heart stack to everyone

loveseeker - a point-blank aoe that gives a heart stack, don't stand in it
	this also spawns moving sets of hearts to dodge
	getting hit gives you a heart stack, so avoid at all costs
love me tender 2 - this time creates many big and small point-blank aoes around the arena randomly
	each one will give a heart stack if you get hit, so dodge everything

blow kiss - a cone aoe towards a random player that gives a heart stack to anyone hit

love me tender 3 - spawns nine point-blank aoes in sequence, with eight around the edge and one in mid
	dodge from the last one into the first

blow kiss

honey b. finale - a raidwide that resets the stage back to normal
