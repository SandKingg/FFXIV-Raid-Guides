wrath of zeus - raidwide

sidewise spark - does a half-room cleave towards the side the boss is indicating

shadows' sabbath x2 - creates a clone at either east or west that casts sidewise spark
	since the cleave will always hit north or south, you can point the boss east or west to help melees keep positional uptime

wicked jolt - a line aoe tankbuster

stampeding thunder - the boss flies north, creating a cannon and blasting five aoes towards where she's aiming
	quickly run to the safe side to avoid the floor disappearing
threefold blast - indicates three aoes on the sides of her cannons, corresponding to the other those sides of the platforms will get hit
	the sides hit will always alternate here
fourfold blast - the same again but this time there are four aoes
	this time there will be most consecutive hits on the same side at one point in the patter, so beware

wicked bolt - a multi-hit stack marker on a random player

bewitching flight - the boss grows wings and flies north with lasers from the wings showing the unsafe parts of the floor
	the wing aoes leave electric lines on the arena that grow and eventually explode
	dodge three sets of puddles during this too
sidewise spark - this time the indicator is on her wings, but it's the same mechanic
