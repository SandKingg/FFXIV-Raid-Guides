uranos cascade - aoe tankbusters on each tank

cronos sling - rotates the cards around him in either a flattened sphere, or a cylinder, indicating a point-blank or doughnut aoe respectively
	then the cards shift either to his left or right, indicating a half-room cleave on that side

empyreal vortex - a muli-hit raidwide that creates ten puddles on the arena and gives spread aoes to random players
	these spreads give magic vulns to make sure not to overlap them

cronos sling - opposite of the previous

warp - teleports to a corner indicated by a circle on the ground and then a pillar of magic
sleepga - a massive cleave in front of him that puts anyone hit by it to sleep
	this can be esuna-ed

gaea stream - four exalines in a 'shuriken' pattern that move through the arena
omega javelin - a spread aoe on random players that leaves a puddle on the ground
	the puddles explode again after a short time, so quickly move out after placing them

duplicate - creates a square aoe that propagates outwards once in all four cardinal directions
	stand in a place that won't be covered by a square
duplicate - this time two squares are created, leaving only one ninth of the arena safe

excelsior + phase shift - a transition cutscene that also removes one ninth of the arena
	the arena now has fully visible tiles for where the squares will be

duplicate + visions of paradise - one of the squares will be swapped with the empty tile
	find the tile that was hit only by the square that is being swapped and stand there

stellar burst - marks a player, dragging them to the centre of the arena and doing a big stack on them
	the aoe of this stack is huge, almost to the edges of the arena

ancient triad - a combination of three elements, each of which can be one of two
	flood/tornado - either a proximity aoe or a pull-in
		the proximity aoe is followed by propagating doughnut aoes from the middle
		the pull-in aoe spawns tornadoes that move through the arena
	shock/flare - either lightning orbs that chase players or fire orbs that do line aoes through the arena
	quake/freeze - turns three tiles either to sand or to ice
gaea stream - this time there is a second set of exalines after the first
