empty salvation - a raidwide that creates six small puddles in a hexagon formation
	standing in them gives you a heavy debuff
fleeting eternity - links four of them together in a Z formation, sending two pink pulses down the branches
	once these reach the end, they explode into large point-blank aoes
fleeting eternity - links to all four of the remaining puddles, sneding a pulse to each
	you can't get melee uptime here :(

wheel of impregnability - summons an orange ring that slowly moves in towards the orange circle, doing a point-blank aoe around the boss once it reaches it

bastion of twilight - summons a green ring that slowly moves in towards the green circle, doing a doughnut aoe around the boss once it reaches it
pestilent penance - jumps to the edge, doing a massive cleave through the arena
	this goes off at the same time as the doughnut

empty salvation 2
fleeting eternity - this time four are linked in the Z pattern and the remaining two are linked by a line
	the line puddles will go off first, followed by the Z puddles
fleeting eternity - connects the final two puddles with a line, doing two more point-blank aoes
comet - aoe tankbusters on each tank

false genesis - add phase, turns the platform into three smaller squares with teleporters in between
	each platform has a unique mechanic to deal with
		winds of promyvion - a rotating line aoe
		empty beleaguer - two sets of two point-blank aoes
		auroral drape - three aoes in the direction the adds are facing
	empty seed - a big knockback from the centre of each platform
		make sure to get knocked into a corner where the walls will prevent you from falling off
deadly rebirth - a raidwide that stuns everyone briefly

empty salvation 3
earthbound heaven - links the six puddles into three pairs of two, placing pink pulses in one puddle in each pair
	the pulses travel to the opposite puddle and explode
	there will always be two pulses placed in two adjacent puddles, so go there to be safe

malevolent blessing - eight small cone aoes from the boss, and a half-room cleave on the glowing side

empty salvation 4 + earthbound heaven - same as 3
bastion of twilight
pestilent penance - this time there are arrows that will do line aoes in the direction they're pointing
	dodge the arrows, doughnut and room cleave

fleeting eternity - links the final two puddles
meteor - aoe tankbusters on each tank and spread aoes on every player
